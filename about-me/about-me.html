Little about me & my background

My name is Saeb Ragani Lamooki. I am from Iran, Mazandaran province (really beautiful). Currently, I am a PhD candidate in Mechanical Engineering program at SUNY at Buffalo (UB) (Buffalo, NY) and I am planning to graduate by the end of Fall 2021. I got my BSc and MSc in Mechanical Engineering from K.N. Toosi U of Tech (KNTU) (Tehran, Iran) and EMU (Magusa, Cyprus). I worked in Shanghai for 2 years and then started my PhD at UB. My research is focused on ergonomic risk assessment on human subjects by analyzing wearable sensor signals using signal processing and time series analysis techniques.

The image gallery is from the mountains my grandparents took their livestock to during Summer.

My home town in north of Iran is trapped by the Caspian sea on the north and the Alborz mountains on the south. This makes it very humid and lush. My grandparents, who grew livestock, had to move to the mountains from late Spring until early Autumn, since the sheeps don't do well in the hot, humid weather. Every year when Summer hit with the school breaks, I fought my folks to let me join them and somtimes I won. Many years later, they are gone and I am left with a ton of great memories. This piece of heaven was my mancave until I left.
